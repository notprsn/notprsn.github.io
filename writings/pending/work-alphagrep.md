## Long write-up

I joined Alphagrep Securities Pvt. Ltd. in May 2022 as a Quantitative Research Intern. I was supposed to go to Optiver this Summer but my VISA got rejected. Funny story, actually. I have no idea why it got rejected. I'd screwed up the Police Verification thing because GOI websites are extremely bad, especially when you have impaired executive functioning. BUT, I managed to get the documents in time for my appointment, and made the appointment which went smoothly (I think). My VISA arrival kept getting delayed till my flight date approached and Dad literally went to the Dutch Embassy in Delhi to press them. I missed the flight, and a couple others and me included had our VISA's rejected a couple days after. I couldn't pattern recognize the reason for the rejections. Shoot 3 random ducks in the bucket, I guess. Or shoot the 3 ugliest ducks in the pond. I don't know. I'm not a duck person.

Luckily, AlphaGrep bit when I started mass applying on LinkedIn to Quant firms who hadn't come for internships. They asked me if I can write code in python and I said sure, I guess. I should've known. The internship was fun, there was just the right amount of impostor syndrome to keep me on my toes. I could sense that I wasn't a cultural fit when I occasionally made social cue blunders. And I'm not remarkably gifted enough to be hired on pure outrageous talent so that was that.

I must've tried the most random trading strategies imaginable here. The idea was take a bunch of historical data, simulate the most random buy sell ideas and flip buys for sells and sells for buys basis a universe of conditions which are totally random. If you have this set-up and play around with the universe of conditions like a monkey given a typewriter, you'll land on 8 sharpe and 14 sharpe. The best part is this outrageous trading strategy set-up actually printed in live too.

In other things, during the internship I unexpectedly made a friend of someone from my department. We did an escape room together and hung out. My AlphaGrep football team also won the AlphaGrep Cup. I got a medal and everything. This was also my first real income, with tax implications and all that jazz. Big whoop.
