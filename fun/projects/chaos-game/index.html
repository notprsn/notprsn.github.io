Fun Math Thing 07

Chaos Game

A regular polygon, one wandering point, and a repeated fractional jump. With three vertices it settles into the familiar triangle; with more vertices and a small rule change, the attractor becomes stranger.

Structural controls reset the trail. Color and guide toggles do not. The point cloud is built incrementally into a persistent offscreen buffer.
