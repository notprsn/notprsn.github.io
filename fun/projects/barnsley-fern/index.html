Fun Math Thing 08

Barnsley Fern

A classic Barnsley fern: four affine transforms, weighted randomness, and enough repeated plotting to coax a full plant out of almost nothing.

The upstream sketch used a shared p5 editor and a simple color cycle. This port keeps the exact fern rules, then gives them a better stage, clearer controls, and a clean regrow loop.
