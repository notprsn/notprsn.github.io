Barnsley Fern

Start with a point. Most of the time, apply the rule that redraws the whole fern a little higher up. Sometimes apply a smaller left leaflet rule, sometimes a right leaflet rule, and rarely the thin stem rule. The choices are random, but the probabilities are not, so the fern slowly assembles itself. The faint points mark the three leaflet anchors and the stem.
