Butterfly Wings

A small upstream p5 study built from one simple idea: let a sinusoid define the wing rhythm, then let Perlin noise keep reshaping the radius until the butterfly never quite repeats itself.

The core port stays faithful to the source: walk around the circle, sample noise, turn that into a radius, and let sin(k * a) fold the curve into wings.
