Brownian Snowflake

A diffusion-limited crystal from the Coding Train challenge: one walker at a time, constrained to a wedge, then mirrored into a full snowflake as the cluster grows.

The core rule stays intact: random drift inward, stick on contact, then rotate and mirror the wedge into a whole crystal.
